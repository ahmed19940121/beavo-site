Important safety

Beavo provides general education and planning support. It does not diagnose conditions and does not replace advice from a midwife, maternity unit, GP or emergency service. For reduced or changed baby movements, bleeding, severe pain, waters breaking with concerning symptoms or any urgent concern, contact the maternity unit or NHS 111. Call 999 in an emergency.

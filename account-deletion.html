Account control

Delete your Beavo account

Signed-in users can start deletion inside Beavo without contacting support.

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Production check required: test this complete route on an actual iPhone against the production Firebase project before publishing this page.

Delete inside the app

Open Profile

Launch Beavo and select Profile in the bottom navigation.

Find Account

Scroll to the Account section. Confirm that the correct Apple or Google account is shown.

Select Delete my account

Read the warning, then confirm permanent cloud-account deletion.

Reauthenticate if asked

For security, Apple or Google may ask the user to sign in again before deletion can finish.

What deletion does

Deleted from Beavo cloud services

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Beavo cloud profile, including preferred name
  • Cloud-synchronised progress and planning data owned by the account
  • Community posts owned by the account, where present

Not automatically removed from the device

  • Guest or local progress remaining in device storage
  • Calendar events already added to Google or Apple Calendar
  • Exported .ics files
  • Support correspondence or limited records retained for legal and security reasons

To clear local Beavo progress, use Reset all progress in Profile or remove the app and its local data. Calendar entries must be removed in the relevant calendar app.

If the app cannot be accessed

Email REPLACE_WITH_SUPPORT_EMAIL with the subject "Beavo account deletion". Include the sign-in provider used and enough information to locate the account. Do not send a password or authentication code. We may request identity verification before acting.

We aim to respond to verified deletion requests within 30 days. In-app deletion is designed to complete immediately when authentication and network requests succeed.

Deletion is permanent: deleted cloud data may not be recoverable. Removing Beavo does not cancel clinical appointments or remove events from an external calendar.
